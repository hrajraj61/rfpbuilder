# 1. Introduction

The AI RFP Intelligence Platform is a SaaS product designed to automate and standardize
the process of analyzing Request for Proposal (RFP) documents for IT service companies,
consulting firms, and system integrators.

The platform transforms unstructured RFP documents into structured technical models
and generates intelligent effort estimates, risk scores, and pricing simulations.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

IT service companies spend significant time manually:

- Reading lengthy RFP documents
- Extracting technical and functional requirements
- Decomposing work into tasks
- Estimating development effort
- Calculating timelines
- Assessing project risks
- Simulating pricing strategies

Manual estimation introduces:

- Underestimation risk
- Margin erosion
- Inconsistent bidding standards
- Reduced win rates
- High dependency on senior architects

There is no structured intelligence layer that converts RFP documents into
data-driven estimation and pricing models.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

Build a cloud-native AI platform that:

1. Ingests RFP documents (PDF/DOCX)
2. Extracts structured requirements using LLM
3. Converts requirements into modules and tasks
4. Maps tasks to an estimation ontology
5. Predicts development effort using ML models
6. Calculates risk scores
7. Simulates pricing and margin scenarios
8. Learns continuously from user feedback

---

# 4. Core Value Proposition

The platform provides:

- 40–60% reduction in bid preparation time
- Standardized estimation framework

- Risk visibility before submission
